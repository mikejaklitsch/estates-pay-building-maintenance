Estates now help pay for building maintenance. Each estate's share is determined by its estate power. Estate-assigned buildings (noble villas, parish churches, guild halls, etc.) are charged in full to their owning estate. Government buildings are assigned to the country and paid in full by the crown.

Building maintenance is raised to 100% of goods market price, up from the vanilla 20% rate. Estates are charged their share directly, so the net cost to the crown is reduced by the total estate power. Foreign buildings follow the same rules as domestic ones.

[h2]Game Rules[/h2]

[b]Estate Building Maintenance[/b] sets the baseline rate to 100% (default), 50%, or 20% (vanilla). [b]Yearly Maintenance Increase[/b] lets you disable the vanilla yearly creep that slowly raises building upkeep over time.

[h2]Compatibility[/h2]

Built for 1.3.*. The mod detects buildings dynamically, so mods that add new buildings are compatible without a patch. Modded fort buildings are the one exception: they work fine, but get folded into the shared maintenance pool and may throw off the numbers slightly. Save-game safe: can be added to or removed from existing saves.
